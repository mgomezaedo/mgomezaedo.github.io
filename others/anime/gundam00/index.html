Mobile Suit Gundam 00

My review and episode guide for Mobile Suit Gundam 00.

Mobile Suit Gundam 00 · 機動戦士ガンダム00（ダブルオー） · ★★★★☆

Studio: Sunrise · 50 Episodes (2 seasons) · 2007–2009

MyAnimeList · AniList

In the distant future, mankind’s dependence on fossil fuels leads to their complete depletion. Three major factions control solar power generators, plunging fossil fuel-dependent nations into poverty and warfare. Amid this chaos, a paramilitary organization called Celestial Being appears, aiming to end all war through armed intervention using Mobile Suits known as Gundams.
